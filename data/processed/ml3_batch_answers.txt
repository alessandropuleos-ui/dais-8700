QUERY 2: What supply chain risks does Apple disclose?
####################################################################################################
Answer:
Apple (AAPL) discloses supply chain risks related to manufacturing and supply chain management, which could affect its ability to obtain components in sufficient quantities on commercially reasonable terms. The company also faces risks from natural disasters, industrial accidents, and cybersecurity attacks that could impact its operations and facilities. Additionally, AAPL relies on contract manufacturers and third-party suppliers, which increases the risk of supply chain disruptions.

QUERY 4: Compare competition risks between Apple and Google.
####################################################################################################
Answer:
Based on the search results from Apple's 10-K filings, it appears that both Apple and Google face significant competition risks in their respective industries. For Apple, this includes competition from companies with established hardware, software, and service offerings, as well as those that imitate its product features and applications. Additionally, Apple faces the risk of losing competitive advantage due to changes in business practices or reputation damage resulting from legal proceedings or government investigations. Similarly, Google's commercial relationships with other companies in the technology industry are subject to investigations and litigation, which could negatively impact its business, results of operations, and financial condition. Overall, both Apple and Google must successfully manage frequent introductions and transitions of products and services to remain competitive and stimulate customer demand.
